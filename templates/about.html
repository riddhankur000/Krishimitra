About Krishimitra

Our mission to empower farmers

Our Mission

Krishimitra is dedicated to empowering farmers across India with transparent, real-time market intelligence. We believe that access to accurate pricing information and market data is fundamental to ensuring fair compensation for farmers' hard work.

Our Vision

A future where every farmer has access to fair market prices and can make informed decisions about their produce, leading to improved livelihoods and sustainable agriculture.

Our Goal

To bridge the information gap between farmers and markets, reduce price exploitation, and create a transparent agricultural ecosystem that benefits all stakeholders.

How We Collect Data

Government Sources

Official mandi data from state agricultural departments

Private Markets

Data partnerships with APMC markets and private traders

Crowdsourcing

Direct input from farmers sharing their sale experiences

Our Partners

We collaborate with various organizations to serve farmers better:

  • State Agricultural Departments - For official mandi data
  • NGOs - Working towards farmer welfare
  • Farmer Cooperatives - Direct farmer organizations
  • Technology Partners - For platform development
